# Topic 2 Exercises

## Exercise 1 - Design Memo

Fill `docs/research_design_memo.md`.

Minimum content:

- research question;
- contribution;
- three references;
- data or model inputs;
- method;
- limitations.

## Exercise 2 - Convert To Issues

Prompt:

```text
Read docs/research_design_memo.md and docs/data_source_map.md. Create five
agent-friendly issues with allowed files, acceptance criteria, dependency labels,
and verification checks. Do not edit files.
```

## Exercise 3 - Vague To Testable

Rewrite:

```text
Make the regression better.
```

as at least three acceptance criteria that mention inputs, outputs, and checks.
