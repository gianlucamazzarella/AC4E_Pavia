# Topic 4 Reading - Replication, Packaging, Presentation

The final package should make it easy for another person to understand what you
tried, what worked, what is blocked, and how to reproduce the evidence.

## Minimum Replication Standard

- clear environment setup;
- clear data access notes;
- exact run command;
- expected outputs;
- known limitations;
- verification evidence.

## Presentation Standard

Your five-to-seven minute presentation should cover:

- research question;
- workflow used;
- what the agent did and what you reviewed;
- result or prototype;
- replication status;
- what you will do in the next 30 days.
